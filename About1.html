About

A Prishtina-based restaurant management company specializing in creating and operating upscale, innovative restaurant concepts, led by Founder and award-winning Chef Ed Gelebus.

Operating since 2005, Flavour is one of the finest restaurants in the Balkans. At Flavour Group, impeccable service is not just our ideal, it is ingrained in everything we do. Rooted deep in our foundation is a selflessness and flexibility that is reflected in an experience wholly centered on others. By embodying our values of adaptability and fluidity, we cultivate a whole new sense of hospitality that is the one thing that makes us the best restaurant in region.
